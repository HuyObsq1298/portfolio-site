About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student with a strong passion for optimal control, flight dynamics, and aerodynamics. After completing two years at HCMUT–VNU, I am transferring to Monash University for the final two years of my Bachelor of Engineering (Honours).

Throughout my studies, I’ve worked on oscillating wave-converter (OWC) systems with Prof. Truong Tich Thien., where I used SolidWorks to design the wave tank and OWC structure, supported meshing, and utilized OpenFOAM (CFD) to assess turbine aerodynamics. Additionally, I briefly touched on microchannel microfluidics involving heat transfer under Assoc. Prof. Le Thanh Long.I mainly use OpenFOAM and Ansys Fluent for simulation, GMSH for grid generation, and Python and Matlab for signal processing and data analysis.
